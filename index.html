About Me



My name is Claudia Castillo. I grew up in Georgia. I graduated from Georgia State University with a Bachelors in Business Administration and a major in Computer Information Systems. Within the IT industry I found a niche in telecoms and worked my way up to a position as a solution architect.


I enjoy updating my skill set with new skills. For example, I found an interest in GIS (Geographical Information Systems), so I obtained a GIS certificate from KSU and retained a job within the Fulton County 911 GIS department. I recently attended KSU project management certificate course to learn the Project Management Body of Knowledge. I am currently attending Georgia Tech Full Stack Web Development certificate course. I am hoping to integrate my previous IT experience and knowledge with this newest skill set and begin a challenging new career as a Full Stack Web Developer.
